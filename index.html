Getting Started

Welcome to MovieStream! Here's how to find your next favorite movie or series:

  1. Select Type: Choose between Movies or Series
  2. Pick Language: Select your preferred language
  3. Choose Platform: (Optional) Select specific OTT platform
  4. Set Genre: (Optional) Pick a genre
  5. Enable Dubbed: (Optional) Check for dubbed versions
  6. Discover: Click "Find My Next Movie" button

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why is a movie not showing?

A: Movies are searched randomly. If unavailable on selected OTT platform, keep searching!

Q: Can I see dubbed versions?

A: Yes! Check "Show only Dubbed" to find movies in your language.

Q: Is my watch history saved?

A: Yes! Each user's history is saved separately and securely.
